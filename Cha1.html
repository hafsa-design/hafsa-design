Grilled Chicken

This Grilled Chicken is flavorful, spicy and perfect for a quick and healthy meal. Marinated in a blend of youghurt, pepper, tandoori masala, salt, this dish is great on its own or served with a rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 pepper
  • 2/4 youghurt
  • 4/4 tandoori masala
  • 1/4 salt

Method

  1. In a bowl, "First, we will put the chicken in a bowl."
  2. "Then, we will apply the spices to the chicken."
  3. "Then, we will add pepper and salt to the chicken."
  4. "Then, we will add yoghurt."
  5. "Then, mix chicken with youghurt and masala very well.
  6. "Then, place it in the fridge for 10 mins.
  7. "Then, place a pan on the stove and add 1/4 cup of olive oil to it.
  8. "Then, we will add the chicken to the pan."
  9. "Then, we need to grill the chicken for 20 minutes."
